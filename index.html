FAQ

  • What is this? Concord is a distributed, encrypted network built to share information, stored within abstracted forms on a block-graph
  • Who wrote this? u2on and LaughableStack
  • Is this an NFT? We the authors belive NFTs should be used to document ownership of tangible goods (e.g. not monkey pictures), but we cannot control the content on Concord; it's entirely user determined
  • When is this available? We're shooting for an MVP by Q1 2022
  • How can I support? You can contribute formats, money, your time, logos, computing power, really anything
