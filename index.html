FAQ + About

  • What is Concord?
    (We're working on a whitepaper at the moment, but this is the gist.)
    Concord is a distributed, decentralised, and encrypted network built to share information and data, stored within abstracted formats on a blockgraph

  • Who created Concord?
    u2on and LaughableStack

  • Is this an NFT?
    Short answer: No
    Long answer: We the authors believe NFTs should be used to document ownership of tangible goods (e.g. not monkey pictures), but we cannot control the content hosted on Concord; it's entirely user determined

  • When is this available?
    We're shooting for an MVP by Q1 2022

  • How can I support?
    Thanks for wanting to support this project! You can contribute custom or ported exchange formats, money, and your time (via code). In the future, we'll set up a way to donate computing power and storage.
