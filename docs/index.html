  • > What is Concord?
    Concord is a distributed, decentralised, and encrypted network built to share information and data, stored within abstracted formats on a blockgraph

  • > Is Concord an NFT?
    No

  • > OS Compatability
    Linux and OpenBSD

  • > When is Concord available?
    While we work on our HME implementation, concord is essentially blocked. The graph structure and networking is complete, so you can run a local graph or with trusted parties.

  • > How can I support Concord?
    You cannot dontate computational power or storage space at the moment. You can contribute to our codebase, or expand our set of protocols.

  • > How can I get in contact?
    Please email "admin [at] openconcord.org" to be added to the /dev mailing list
