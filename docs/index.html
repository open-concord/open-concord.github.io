FAQ + About

  • > What is Concord?

    (We're working on a whitepaper at the moment, but this is the gist.)
    Concord is a distributed, decentralised, and encrypted network built to share information and data, stored within abstracted formats on a blockgraph

  • > Who created Concord?

    u2on (website) and LaughableStack

  • > Is Concord an NFT?

    Short answer: No
    Long answer: We the authors believe NFTs should be used to document ownership of tangible goods. However, due to the nature of Concord, we cannot control the content hosted or propagated through its protocol(s). OpenConcord has no offical NFT or coin

  • > When is Concord available?

    We're shooting for an MVP by Q3 2022

  • > How can I support Concord?

    Thanks for wanting to support this project!
    Right now, you can contribute via our github. In the future, we'll set up a way to donate computing power and storage.
